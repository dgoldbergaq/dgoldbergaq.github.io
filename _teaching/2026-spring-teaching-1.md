This course explores “thinking sustainably” from the perspective of environmental health and our energy-intensive lifestyle. Technical, socio-political, and health implications of each fossil fuel and renewable energy source are discussed in the context of broader issues such as air pollution, water quality, climate change, and the importance of maintaining a healthy environment for future generations.
